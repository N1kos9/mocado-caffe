Our Story

Mocado Caffe S.R.L., founded in 2014, has become a trusted brand in the ROCT market after 10 years of activity. Offering a special experience, combining relaxation, science, and innovation, Mocado Caffe brings diversity and quality at affordable prices.

We inspire the senses and create a place where customers can connect, share memories, and always discover something new. Mocado Caffe is the source of energy for daily plans, offering truly remarkable drinks in a special selection.
